We Handle Everything

PrEP (pre-exposure prophylaxis) is a once-a-day medication that, when taken correctly, is 99% effective in stopping the spread of HIV, helping people who are HIV negative stay HIV negative.

Q Care+ makes getting on PrEP quick, easy, and affordable with free online doctor visits, free at-home labs (including STI testing), and free delivery of your prescription discreetly to your door. When it comes to the medication, insured or uninsured, we will help you qualify for various patient assistance programs so you have no out pocket costs.

Our team of experts will take care of all the paperwork so that you don’t have to worry about anything.

PrEP Can Be Totally Free

Because why pay if you don't have to?

99% of Q Care+ members pay $0 for their medication, and we make sure the online doctor visits and the labs required for being on PrEP are also free. We'll also take care of all the paperwork, so you don’t have to worry about a thing.

If You're Insured...

If you have health insurance, we’ll work to get your prescription for Descovy or Truvada covered by your provider. We can also help enroll you for Gilead’s co-pay card that, for many people, pays up to 100% of their out-of-pocket expenses for PrEP.

If You're Uninsured...

No worries. If you don’t have health insurance, are underinsured, or if you are an undocumented immigrant, we’ll help get you enrolled in Gilead’s Advancing Access program, which covers just about everything
